Baramati

District-Pune, Maharashtra State, India

Baramati city is in Pune District of Maharashrea State in India. Baramati is recognised as Loksabha Constituency domonated by Minister Sharad Pawar. At present Mr.Sharad Pawar is Member of Parliament nominated for Council of Ministers. His daughter Mrs.Supriya Sule is an elected member of House of People in Parliament of India.

Climate in Baramati is humid due to which summar is not intense. There have been satisfactory rain fall in the region around Baramati. Due to which there is no scarcity of water even during summer.
